All industries get impacted in some way or another during major world events such as recessions. However, the aviation industry is one of the industries that get impacted the most when a major crisis occurs. This section covers some of the most impactful crises in the last 30 years.

  • 9/11 Terrorist Attacks
  • Iraq War
